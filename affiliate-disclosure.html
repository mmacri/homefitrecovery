Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about our affiliate partnerships and how we make money

Last Updated: January 15, 2023

TLDR (Too Long; Didn't Read)

Recovery Essentials participates in affiliate programs, meaning we may earn commissions when you click on our links and make purchases. This comes at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products we genuinely believe in, and affiliate relationships never influence our reviews or recommendations.

Full Disclosure

Recovery Essentials ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to transparency in everything we do. In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines, we want to make it clear to our readers that we participate in various affiliate marketing programs.

This means that when you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the sale. These affiliate relationships include, but are not limited to, Amazon Associates Program, and various direct partnerships with manufacturers and retailers in the recovery product space.

How Affiliate Links Work

When you click on an affiliate link on our website, a small piece of code tracks that you came from our site. If you make a purchase during that session, or sometimes within a specified timeframe, we receive a small percentage of the sale as a commission. The price you pay remains exactly the same whether you use our affiliate links or go directly to the vendor's website.

Our Commitment to You

We want to be clear about several important points regarding our affiliate relationships:

  • Editorial Independence: We maintain a strict separation between our editorial team and business operations. Product reviews, recommendations, and rankings are determined solely by our testing process and editorial standards, not by affiliate partnerships or commission potential.
  • Product Selection: We often recommend products that earn us no commission if we genuinely believe they are the best option for our readers. Our first priority is always providing accurate, helpful information.
  • Transparency: We include affiliate disclosure notices throughout our website to ensure you're always aware when links may generate revenue for us.
  • No Additional Cost: Using our affiliate links never costs you anything extra. In fact, we occasionally negotiate exclusive discounts for our readers.

Our Product Testing Process

We purchase all products we review at retail prices. We do not accept free products or samples from manufacturers to ensure our reviews remain completely unbiased. This way, we experience the same customer journey that you would - from purchase to delivery to long-term use.

Our detailed testing methodologies involve both objective and subjective criteria, and we document key performance metrics, user experiences, and long-term durability. You can read more about our testing process on our About Us page.

Additional Revenue Streams

In addition to affiliate commissions, Recovery Essentials may generate revenue through:

  • Display Advertising: We display advertisements on our website, which are clearly marked as such.
  • Premium Content: We may offer paid membership options that provide access to exclusive content, personalized recommendations, or other benefits.

These additional revenue streams help support our extensive testing processes and allow us to maintain our commitment to purchasing all reviewed products at retail.

Changes to This Disclosure

We may update this Affiliate Disclosure from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or affiliations. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date at the top will be updated accordingly.
